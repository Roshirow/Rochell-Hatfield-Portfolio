Role

Humane Society Website Design: Increasing Adoptions Through Strategic UX

Challenge: Animal shelter needed a website that would increase adoption rates by addressing common user barriers (behavioral concerns, fee anxieties) while optimizing donation flows to sustain operations.

Approach: Conducted comprehensive user research using AVMA demographic data, competitive analysis of Washington state shelters, and stakeholder interviews. Developed user personas and journey maps based on data showing that 15% of non-adopters avoided shelters due to behavioral concerns. Designed information architecture that proactively addresses misconceptions, streamlines the adoption process, and makes donations frictionless.

Key insights: Research revealed that individual donations are the primary revenue source for shelters, and average shelter stays were 29.6 days for dogs and 45.5 days for cats. Designed dual focus on adoption conversion AND donor optimization to support organizational sustainability.
